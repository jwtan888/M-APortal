How We Classify Skill Level

Each completed template job is tagged with a complexity level. A PIC's Capability Index uses all completed jobs and gives rarer complexity levels more influence, while Work Mix is the normal all-job average. Use the table below to understand what each capability tier means.

💡

Capability Index weights each completed job by the inverse of that level's share of team jobs; fewer than 10 jobs is shown as insufficient evidence. A PIC is considered Advanced when their Capability Index reaches 2.6 or above, meaning their completed evidence includes meaningful Level 3+ work after adjusting for the scarcity of Level 4–5 opportunities. Reaching Expert (3.6+) requires consistent delivery of multi-operation work. Expert+ (4.5+) requires sustained Level 5 machine-modification and programming evidence.
